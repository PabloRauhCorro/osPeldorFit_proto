SPIN SYSTEM
--------------------------------------
Nitroxide-nitroxide model compound (compound #1 in the references given below).


PDS DATASET

References:
G.W. Reginsson, R.I. Hunter, P.A.S. Cruickshank, D.R. Bolton, S.T. Sigurdsson, G.M. Smith, and O. Schiemann, 
J. Magn. Reson. 216, 175 (2012);
D. Abdullin, G. Hagelueken, R. I. Hunter, G.M. Smith, O. Schiemann,
Mol. Phys. 2015, 113, 544-560.
