SPIN SYSTEM
--------------------------------------
Low-spin iron(III)-trityl model compound (compound 1T*Im2 in the reference given below).


PDS DATASET
--------------------------------------
A Q-band RIDME time trace.

References:
D. Abdullin, P. Brehm, N. Fleck, S. Spicher, S. Grimme, O. Schiemann,
Chem. Eur. J. 2019, 25, 14388-14398.
